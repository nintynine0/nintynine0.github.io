My biography

Hi! I’m Yanlan Hu, in my final year as a graduate student in University of Science and Technology of China (USTC).

Research interest

My research interests focus on earthquake triggering, the evolution of foreshocks, mainshocks and aftershocks. I am interested in fine-scale earthquake sequence analysis based on fault geometry. I also do research in machine learning in seismic monitoring, clustering and data mining.

Besides, I am also interested in earthquake physics and hazard early warning.

Other

I love photographing and various sports. I love trying new things.
